Wood Floor Sanding & Re-finishing

Fellow Mister Group member FloorFixer is the only Leicestershire based flooring company that is exclusively dedicated to wood floor renovation. They are not floor fitters, but specialise in wood floor restoration using the latest technology in low-dust floor sanding machines which are suitable for all types of wood flooring, including old parquet.

The wood floor repair and sanding process is carried out before refinishing with either hard wax oil or lacquer (varnish) to seal the surface, which then transforms your room from being drab and lifeless - into a beautiful space to live in.

Wood flooring projects are always associated with stress because of the expected dust and mess. The combined experience of the FloorFixer team has overcome this by developing a process designed to overcome this inconvenience. To achieve this, Floorfixer use professional equipment and protective wood floor finishes with the latest technology to achieve unprecedented results every time.
