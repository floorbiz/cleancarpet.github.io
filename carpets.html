Carpet Cleaning 

The Steam Cleaning Specialists
Most carpet manufacturers agree that Steam Cleaning with a hot water extraction system is the most efficient method for removing dirt deep down inside your carpet. The efficiency of the process is aided by using boiling hot water in our powerful hot water extraction machine. It forces a steamspray into the carpet under high pressure, before immediately sucking it back into the unit again together with soiling from the carpet. The steam penetrates into the base of the fibres and releases stubborn dirt which may be trapped within the fibres of the carpet pile.

Steam cleaning is also considered to be the healthiest way of having your carpet cleaned, because the high water temperature destroys bacteria, fungus, mold and dust mites. 'DRY' carpet-cleaning may leave detergent residues which can subsequently attract dirt!!
