Upholstery Cleaning 

Upholstery looking a bit grubby?
Most people do not realise that upholstery fabrics come into more contact with our bodies than carpets. They can become smelly from perspiration or greasy from hair and cosmetics. A Sofa or arm-chair is one of the most expensive items you will ever purchase for your home so it is important to ensure that these valuable posessions stay clean and fresh in your home.

Upholstery fabrics with fine colours or materials require a far more delicate technique with steam cleaning than carpets, and having the right knowledge is vital. Different tools, equipment and products are used and we carefully check beforehand for any hidden problems or issues which could arise from steam cleaning the fabric. We also carry out colour-fast and other appropriate tests before starting any work.

Our customers can rest assured that the upholstery covering their furniture will be cleaned safely and efficiently. Professional upholstery cleaning from Leicester Carpet Cleaners will make certain that we never damage your upholstery fabric. Most fabrics are fine, but if there is a risk of damage, we will let you know and even decline the project if necessary. We would rather not jeopardise your upholstery...or our great reputation!
